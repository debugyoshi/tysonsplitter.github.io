To control:

Enter your segment time into the active segment beside your opponent. It will update automatically
when you click elsewhere, so make sure what you entered is correct! Splits will be calculated
gradually as each segment is added. When your run is complete and you would like to save your run if
a personal best was acheived, press the "End Run" button. Your PB Segments and best time
will be updated accordingly.

If at any time your segment is better than your best segment, your old best segment will be replaced
with the new best. Please note all saved time and bests data is stored in your browser data.
Clearing cache will clear any PB data, so make sure to download your segments often! NOTE:
UPLOADING new segments will OVERWRITE ALL old segments, including PB and best times, so be
careful!

Any improvement suggestions? New world record appeared? Please open an issue on the github
repository, which you can reach by clicking here.
